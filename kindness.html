Kindness

Somebody thought your act of kindness was meaningful enough to give you this card. More than likely the person you were kind to was pleased and passed on your kindness, perhaps in their own way, to someone else. The person who gave you this card was certainly pleased, and as a card holder also passed on the kindness.

That's at least four people you randomly made happy today.

Four people, one of whom might have been having suicidal thoughts, but saw your gesture as a sign and decided not to take their life today.

One of those people might have been on their way home to take their anger out on their child by beating them, but your kindness no longer made them angry.

Sometimes a simple interaction can make a difference to someone in ways that noone will ever know. Maybe your ten second interaction delayed a person just enough that they weren't in an accident with the person who ran that red light.

Or maybe they weren't hit by that stray bullet.

Or maybe they were half a block away when that tree fell over in the wind storm, instead of being directly under it.

We'll never know...

Hopefully this will inspire you to contine to do good things for others.
